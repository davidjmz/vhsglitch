Synthesized music inspired by the 80's and early 90's.

Biography

VHS Glitch is a Synthwave/Darkwave/Retrowave artist, Graphic Designer and Videogame soundtrack composer. Who began in 2014 with his single Digital Rebirth.

VHS Glitch released 4 albums until now. They are Night On The Dark Hill, Halloween Strangers, Evil Technology, and his last one, Land With No Future.

Those albums were released in physical format soon or later after the digital release, but with a little number of copies.

Last physical releases

  • 10/02/2016 - Halloween Strangers, casette, 50 copies. (Sold out after 2 hours and 44 minutes)
  • 17/12/2015 - Land With No Future, vinyl, 200 copies.
  • 25/08/2015 - Evil Technology, casette, 100 copies.
